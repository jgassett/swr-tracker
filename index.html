How a job works

Every job moves through the same steps — follow the arrows through the grid.

👥
Customer — find or add the client and their property. Every job hangs off a customer.
💼
Jobs — open a job for the engagement and manage its tasks from set to pull.
📅
Schedule — see the crew's tasks on the calendar and mark them complete as you go.
📷
Monitoring — check trap site cameras and respond to activity and health alerts.
🦝
Field Work — log every capture, release, or pesticide treatment while you're on site: the Trapping and Pesticides tabs live here. Estimates live in the bottom bar; invoicing happens in QuickBooks Online.
✅
Close Out — once every task on a job is completed or cancelled, close it out here. The job moves to Complete and billing continues in QuickBooks.
