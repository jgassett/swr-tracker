Privacy Policy

SWR Tracker — operated by Southern Wildlife Resources LLC
Last updated: July 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Southern Wildlife Resources LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects information in connection with SWR Tracker (the “App”), a private field-tracking and customer-management application used by our authorized operators for nuisance-wildlife control and pesticide-application recordkeeping.

SWR Tracker is an internal business tool. It is not marketed to or made available to the general public, and it is not directed to children.

1. Information we collect

  • Operator account information — the email address and authentication credentials of each authorized operator who signs in to the App.
  • Customer directory records — customer name, phone number, email address, street address, city, county, and free-form notes entered by our operators or imported from our accounting system.
  • Service records — trapping and pesticide-application details such as dates, locations, species, products used, and related field notes.
  • QuickBooks data (when connected) — when an authorized administrator connects our QuickBooks Online company, the App performs a one-way, read-only import of our customer list (customer name, phone, email, and billing address) to populate the App’s customer directory. The App does not write to, modify, or delete any data in QuickBooks, and it does not access financial, invoice, payroll, or banking data.

2. How we use information

Information in the App is used solely to operate our business — scheduling and documenting wildlife-control and pesticide services, maintaining our customer directory, and meeting recordkeeping and regulatory-reporting obligations. We do not sell, rent, or trade any information, and we do not use it for advertising.

3. How information is stored and secured

App data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, and Cloud Storage), operated on Google Cloud Platform infrastructure. Access requires operator authentication. Our QuickBooks connection credentials are stored server-side in Google Cloud Secret Manager and Firestore locations that are not readable by App users; they are never exposed in the browser. Data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections.

4. Third-party services

The App relies on the following service providers, whose handling of data is governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase / Google Cloud Platform — application hosting, database, authentication, and file storage.
  • Intuit QuickBooks Online — the source of the read-only customer import described above, used only when an administrator connects our QuickBooks company.

We share information with these providers only as necessary to operate the App.

5. Data retention

We retain records for as long as needed to run our business and to satisfy applicable legal, regulatory, and recordkeeping requirements. Records may be deleted or de-identified when no longer needed.

6. Your choices and rights

Because SWR Tracker is an internal tool, individuals whose information appears in our records may contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of their information, subject to our legal and recordkeeping obligations. An administrator may disconnect the QuickBooks integration at any time, which stops all further import of customer data.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
